5 - Birth

出生

Gabriel cleared his throat and raised his baton.

加布裡埃爾清了清嗓子，舉起了他的指揮棒。


The angelic hosts of Heaven stopped chatting and rustling

天堂的天使們停止了聊天和沙沙聲


and stood invisibly to attention in the darkened sky,

它們筆直地矗立在昏暗的夜空中，隱形無蹤，


as unsuspecting shepherds in the fields below

而下面田野裡的一些毫無猜的牧羊人


sat quietly together around a small fire,

一起安靜地圍著一小塊火，


their sheep huddled close in the snowy fields.

他們的羊群蜷縮在雪地裡。


💠



In the even darker stable,

在更黑暗的馬廄裡，


Mary perspired and gasped and groaned,

馬利亞渾身是汗，氣喘吁籲，呻吟，


as her body laboured to expel

當她的身體努力排出時


the holy, precious parcel within.

裡面神聖而珍貴的包裹。


💠



If He could have, Jesus would have cried out --

如果他可以的話，耶穌會喊——


but He couldn't.

但他做不到。


After a long, relaxing stay

經過長時間的放鬆停留


in a dark, cosy, comforting environment --

在一個黑暗、溫馨、舒適的環境中——


during which He had learned to kick and move about in a limited way,

在此期間，他學會了以有限的方式踢和移動，


His every need supplied through a fleshy tube connected to His little belly --

他的每一個需求都透過連線到他的小肚子的肉管來供應——


everything was changing!

一切都在改變！


The walls of His safe, soft, friendly home were closing in on Him,

他安全、柔軟、友好的家的牆壁正在逼近他，


squeezing Him,

擠壓他，


as if He were suddenly --

彷彿他突然——


no longer welcome.

不再歡迎。


He was going somewhere --

他要去某個地方——


His little head being bent out of shape!

他的小頭現在正被掰彎變形呢！


It was all so unexpected and terrifyingly strange,

這一切都出人意料，而且非常奇怪，


He didn't have time to ask His Father

他沒時間問他父親


what was happening.

到底是怎麼回事。


💠



The moment Jesus' head popped out,

耶穌的頭出來的那一刻，


Gabriel pointed at the specially designed star angel,

加布裡埃爾指著特別設計的星星天使，


who now rose brilliantly --

現在已經崛起，光芒四射——


twinkling with every colour of the rainbow!

閃爍著彩虹的每一種顏色！


He gave the momentous, joyous,

他給了重要的，快樂的，


reassuring

安慰的


announcement,

公告，


as shepherds and sheep

作為牧羊人和羊群


fell over one another

互相摔倒


in startled amazement.

驚愕不已。


Now Gabriel lifted his baton,

現在加布裡埃爾舉起了他的指揮棒，


and the full angelic choir appeared, infusing the entire sky with a glorious light,

完整的天使合唱團出現了，為整個天空注入了燦爛的光芒，


singing beautifully,

優美地唱歌，


melodiously,

旋律美妙地，


with unquenchable joy.

帶著不竭的喜悅。


💠



Meanwhile, in the darkened stable below,

與此同時，在下面黑暗的馬廄裡，


as soon as His tiny lungs

當他小小的肺


filled

吸入


with their first gasp of air,

第一口空氣時，


Baby Jesus...

耶穌寶寶......


cried.

哭了。


💠



Based on John 1:1-2,14; Luke 2:1-14; Matthew 2:1-2,9-10.

基於約翰福音1:1-2,14；路加福音2:1-14；馬太福音2:1-2,9-10。

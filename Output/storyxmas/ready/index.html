3 - Ready

準備好

At last, it was Time.

終於到了時間。


There was much hustle and bustle in Heaven,

天堂裡有很多熙熙攘攘，


as the entire angelic host added the final flourishes

當全體天使加入最後的點睛之筆時


to the glorious, melodious song they were to sing

他們要唱的那首光榮、悠美的歌曲


for a few shepherds and their sheep

為了幾個牧羊人和他們的羊


in a field at night...!

晚上在田野裡......！


"Really?!"

「真的嗎？！」


Gabriel had asked incredulously.

加百列不置信地問道。


"All this for a few shepherds and some sheep, my Lord?!"

「這一切都是為了幾個牧羊人和一些羊，我的主啊？！」


"Really."

「真的。」


The Father had replied

父親已經回答了


with an amused,

帶著一個玩味的，


affectionate smile.

深情的微笑。


💠



One especially beautiful, delightfully bright angel --

一個特別美麗、令人愉悅的明亮的天使——


designed expressly for this unprecedented moment

專門為這個前所未有的時刻而設計


in all of human and Heavenly History --

在所有人類和天堂的歷史中——


was set to rise --

被設定為上升——


a brilliant new star, high in the sky --

一顆明亮的新星，高高在天上——


directly over the small, dark, ramshackle stable in Bethlehem

就在伯利恆的小而黑暗、破的馬廄上方


where Jesus would be born --

耶穌將在哪裡出生——


an unmistakable sign for stargazers

對於觀星者來說，這是一個明確無誤的訊號


of the momentous birth.

重大的誕生。


💠



Now the Father looked at Jesus expectantly.

現在，天父期待地看著耶穌。


Taking a deep breath, Jesus stood,

耶穌深吸一口氣，站著，


and bowed low

深深地鞠了一躬


before nestling into His Father's powerful, protective Arms

在依偎在他父親強大、保護的手臂之前


for a prolonged embrace.

為了長時間的擁抱。


He didn't want to leave Heaven

他不想離開天堂


but He would...

但他會......


out of Love.

因為愛。


💠



Based on Luke 2:1-14; Matthew 2:1-10; John 1:1-5,14,18; Luke 1:31-35; John 3:16.

基於路加福音2:1-14；馬太福音2:1-10；約翰福音1:1-5,14,18；路加福音1:31-35；約翰福音3:16。

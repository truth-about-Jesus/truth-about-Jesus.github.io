31 - Found

被找到

"Those men -- the ones you put in prison --

「那些人——你關進監獄的那些人——


they're standing in the Temple,

他們站在聖殿裡，


teaching the people!"

教導人民！」


Silence.

沉默。


The Chief Priest's servant wondered if the Council had heard him.

祭司長的僕人想知道理事會是否聽到了他的話。


He was about to repeat himself more loudly --

他正要更大聲地重複自己——


when the entire Council erupted in a hubbub of dismay.

當整個理事會爆發出沮喪的喧鬧聲時。


"What?!"

「什麼？！」


"That's impossible!"

「那是不可能的！」


"How?!"

「怎麼？！」


"Did someone release them?"

「有人釋放了他們嗎？」


"The guards must have fallen asleep!"

「警衛們肯定睡著了！」


And, most importantly:

而且，最重要的是：


"Now, what?"

「現在，什麼？」


💠



The truth was, for all their show

事實是，儘管他們表面上如此裝腔作勢


of authority and power,

權威和權力，


the Chief Priest and the entire Ruling Council were deathly afraid

祭司長和整個統治理事會都害怕得要死


of the people,

人民的，


who were following the new movement like dimwitted sheep.

他們像一隻笨羊一樣跟隨著新運動。


The rulers were well aware that the Galilean rabbi -- whom they had condemned to death -- had been revered by many as a prophet from God.

統治者們很清楚，他們判處死刑的那位加利利教員，被許多人視為上帝的先知。


And now the rabbi's followers were rising in popularity --

現在那教員的追隨者越來越受歡迎——


especially

尤其


with the recent, very public healing

隨著最近的、非常公開的奇蹟般的治癒


of the lame man -- Azariah -- not long ago.

不久前，那個瘸子——阿札裡亞。


It was fine to arrest such deceivers under cover of darkness...

在黑暗中逮捕這樣的騙子是可以的......


but a wholly different matter to arrest them in broad daylight,

但在光天化日之下逮捕他們是完全不同的事情，


before the very eyes of the people

在人們的眼前


who held them in such high esteem.

誰如此尊重他們。


💠



At last, not willing to appear as fools or cowards before the Temple guards and servants,

最後，不願意在聖殿守衛和僕人面前表現為傻瓜或懦夫，


the entire Ruling Council rose, together with their attendants,

整個統治理事會及其隨從都站了起來，


and headed toward the Temple en masse,

並集體走向聖殿，


hoping against hope

希望


the people would hesitate

人們會猶豫不決


to stone

用石刑


such a large group.

在對付這麼大的群。

基於使徒行傳3:1-10；5:25-26。

38 - The scheme

狡計

"People are selling houses and fields,

「人們正在出售房屋和田地，


and bringing the whole proceeds

並帶來全部收入


to the Apostles," remarked Ananias to his wife,

給使徒們，」阿納尼亞斯對他的妻子說，


a troubled look on his face.

他臉上露出憂心忡忡的表情。


The couple were two of many in Jerusalem

這對夫婦是耶路撒冷眾多人中的兩個人


who had come to believe in Jesus,

開始信奉耶穌，


the resurrected Messiah.

復活的彌賽亞。


"Oh...!"

「哦......！」


Sapphira pondered, then said reluctantly:

薩菲拉沉思著，然後不情願地說：


"I suppose we ought to do the same..."

「或許我們也應該這樣做......」


💠



"Look how much I got for our field!"

「看看我為我們的田地得到了多少錢！」


Ananias came in,

阿納尼亞斯進來了，


his hands filled with shekels.

他手裡拿著滿滿的錢。


"Old Abel wanted to expand his farm,

「老亞伯想擴大他的農場，


and I pretended we were reluctant to sell...

我假裝我們不願意賣......


and look at the bargain I made!"

看看我做的討價還價！」


He dumped the shekels on the table,

他把錢倒在桌上，


then pulled even more out of his pockets,

然後從他的口袋裡掏出更多，


as Sapphira looked on, astonished.

薩菲拉驚訝地看著。


"You always were a hard bargainer," she laughed in amazement.

「你一直都很會討價還價，」她笑驚豔說。。


"Why, this is more than double what that field is worth!"

「嗬，這是那個領域價值的兩倍多！」


💠



Ananias savoured his wife's praise, then pondered a moment.

阿納尼亞斯品味著妻子的讚美，然後思考了一會兒。


"You know, I think it would be quite believable

「你知道，我認為這很可信


if we only gave half...

如果我們只給一半......


Just the other day I saw Joe Barnabas

就在前幾天，我看到了喬·巴納巴斯


bring in the proceeds of the sale of his field,

帶來出售他田地的收益，


and it was only..." he named a paltry sum.

這只是......」他說出了一個小的金額。


"That's all?!"

「僅此而已？！」


Sapphira shook her head in disbelief.

薩菲拉難以置信地搖了搖頭。


Joe Barnabas did not have one-tenth the business acumen of her husband!

喬·巴納巴斯的商業頭腦沒有她丈夫的十分之一！

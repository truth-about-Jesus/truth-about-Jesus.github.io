32 - Invitation

邀請

John looked up, then nudged Peter.

約翰抬起頭來，然後推了推彼得。


They and the other Apostles were sitting in the Temple, as their Master had done,

他們和其他使徒坐在聖殿裡，就像他們的主人所做的那樣，


speaking His words of life

把生命的道講給


to the people sitting around them --

給坐在他們周圍的人——


just as the angel

就像那個天使一樣


who'd released them from prison

誰把他們從監獄裡釋放的


had commanded.

已經命令了。


Peter looked up.

彼得抬起頭。


A large group of well-dressed elders and rulers -- actually, the entire Ruling Council! -- led by the Chief Priest and accompanied by guards and attendants,

一大群衣著整禮的長老和統治者——實際上是整個統治理事會！——祭司長在前面，由警衛和隨從陪同，


was heading their way.

正朝著他們的方向前進。


💠



"Excuse me."

「打擾一下。」


It galled the Chief Priest to have to speak so politely

祭司長不得不如此禮貌地說話，這讓他感到很惱大


to illiterate commoners,

給文盲的平民，


but he didn't dare risk the anger of the huge crowd surrounding them --

但他不敢冒著他們周圍一大群人的憤怒的風險——


some of whom were beginning to stand up --

他們中的一些人開始站起來了——


rather threateningly, Caiaphas thought.

相當具有威脅性，該亞法想。


"We would like to invite you, the chosen followers of --" he coughed, "Jesus of Nazareth, to a hearing of the Ruling Council,

「我們想邀請你們，被選中的追隨者——」他咳嗽道，「拿撒勒的耶穌，參加執政委員會的聽證會，


so we may discuss together,

所以我們可以一起討論，


in a civil, collaborative manner,

以文明、協作的方式，


what you are doing."

你在做什麼。」


💠



As the Holy Spirit nudged his heart to accede to the request of the man

當聖靈推動他的心同意這個人的請求時


who had only recently imprisoned

那個不久前才把他們關進監獄的人


him and the others

他和其他人


for days,

幾天來，


the words of the Master echoed

大師的話迴盪著


quietly but persistently

安靜但堅持不懈地


through John's mind:

透過約翰的頭腦：


"Do not resist an evil person.

「不要反抗惡人。


If someone slaps you on the right cheek,

若有人打你的右臉，


turn to him the other also.

讓他們也打你的另一邊臉頰。


If someone forces you to go one mile with him,

若有人強逼你走一里路，


go two.

你就同他走二里。


Whoever demands of you,

如果有人向你索取東西，


give to him;

就給他；


whoever wants to borrow from you,

有向你借貸的，


do not refuse."

不推辭。」


💠



"Love your enemies!

「愛你們的仇敵！


Bless the one who curses you!

祝福詛咒你的人！


Do good to the one who hates you.

善待恨你們的人


Pray for those

為那些的禱告


who lead you in chains

為用鎖鏈捆綁你們的人，


and persecute you."

為那些迫害你們的人。」


💠



Peter, John and the rest of the Apostles

彼得、約翰和其他使徒


got up quietly,

安靜地起床了，


and, without protest,

並且，沒有抗議，


followed the Ruling Council

跟隨統治理事會


back into the Council Hall.

回到理事會大廳。


💠



Based on Acts 5:17-26; Matthew 5:38-45; Luke 6:27-30.

基於使徒行傳5:17-26；馬太福音5:38-45；路加福音6:27-30。

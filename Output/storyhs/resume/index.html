33 - Hearing resumed

聽證會恢復

"Lock the doors!"

「把門鎖上！」


Caiaphas tried to keep the sense of urgency out of his voice

該亞法試圖將緊迫感從他的聲音中排除在外


as the entire Ruling Council hustled safely back into the Council Hall

當整個統治理事會安全地趕回理事會大廳時


with their unusually compliant prisoners.

和他們異常順從的囚犯。


No sense in taking any chances.

冒險沒有任何意義。


💠



As the Council settled back into their seats and rearranged their robes,

當理事會回到他們的座位上並重新安排他們的長袍時，


the Apostles stood quietly in the midst.

使徒們靜靜地站在中間。


They had not intended to escape...

他們本不打算逃跑......


but what else could one do --

但人們還能做什麼——


faced with an angel sent by God?

面對上帝派來的天使？


Deep down inside they had known:

內心深處，他們知道：


the Ruling Council,

統治理事會，


like a dog with a bone,

像一條有骨頭的狗，


would not give up so easily.

不會那麼容易放棄。


💠



Once he had sufficiently recovered from their bold venture, the Chief Priest bristled with indignation.

一旦他從他們大膽的冒險中充分恢復過來，首席牧師就充滿了憤慨。


"Did we not strictly charge you," he began severely, "not to teach anyone in this name?"

「我們不是嚴格命令你嗎，」他嚴厲地開始說，「不要以這個名義教任何人？」


He hated to think -- let alone speak -- the name of the false Messiah.

他討厭思考——更不用說說話了——假彌賽亞的名字。


"And now, look! You've filled Jerusalem with your teaching;

「現在，看！ 你用你的教誨填滿了耶路撒冷；


and you want to blame us

你想責怪我們


for the blood of this man!"

為了這個人的血！」


💠



As the Apostles looked quietly back at their accusers,

當使徒們靜靜地回頭看他們的指控者時，


enjoying the flow of supernatural peace through their hearts and minds,

透過他們的心靈和思想享受超自然和平的流動，


certain ones sensed the Holy Spirit rising within,

某些人感覺到聖靈在內心升起，


giving them the words

給他們話語


with which to respond.

回應。

基於使徒行傳5:17-28。

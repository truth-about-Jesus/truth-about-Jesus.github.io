34 - Fury

暴怒

"We must obey God rather than men,"

「我們必須服從上帝，而不是人，」


the Holy Spirit explained patiently to the Council through Peter,

聖靈透過彼得耐心地向理事會解釋，


then gently, yet clearly, through Thomas:

然後溫和地，但清晰地，透過托馬斯：


"The God of our fathers has resurrected Jesus

「我們祖先的上帝復活了耶穌


whom you killed

你殺了他


when you crucified Him on the Cross."

當你把他釘在十字架上時。」


John was next:

約翰是下一個：


"God has appointed Him

「上帝已經任命了他


Prince and Saviour,

王子和救世主，


and exalted Him to His right hand,

將他高舉在自己的右邊，


to give repentance and forgiveness of sins to Israel."

讓以色列悔改和赦免罪。」


And finally, through James:

最後，透過詹姆斯：


"We are witnesses of these things,

「我們是這些事情的見證人，


as is the Holy Spirit Himself,

就像聖靈本身一樣，


whom God has given

上帝賜予的靈


to those who believe in Him."

給那些相信他的人。」


💠



The nuance did not escape the Chief Priest, who leapt to his feet in fury.

細微差別並沒有逃脫首席牧師，他憤怒地跳了起來。


"Are you insinuating that we do not believe?

「你是在暗示我們不相信嗎？


We,

我們，


the guardians of the Law of Moses,

摩西律法的守護者，


the Temple,

聖殿，


and all the traditions of the elders?!"

以及長輩們的所有傳統？！」


He spoke as fiercely as he could,

他儘可能地激烈地說話，


as many other Council members, enraged, jumped to their feet.

當許多其他理事會成員憤怒地站起來時。


💠



As calls for execution -- even crucifixion! -- echoed throughout the Council Hall,

隨著處決的呼聲——甚至是釘十字架！——在整個理事會廳迴盪，


Nicodemus exchanged a worried glance with Joseph of Arimathea.

尼哥底母與亞利馬太的約瑟夫交換了擔憂的目光。


It was happening

它正在發生


again.

再次。

Based on Acts 5: 27-33.

基於使徒行傳5：27-33。

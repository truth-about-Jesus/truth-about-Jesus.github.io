36 - Decision

決定

Both Nicodemus and Joseph breathed grateful prayers of relief

尼哥底母和約瑟都鬆了一口氣，滿懷感恩地禱告


as Gamaliel's calming influence began to take effect across the Council Hall.

當迦瑪列的鎮靜影響開始在整個理事會大廳生效時。


Could he possibly be

他有可能是


a secret believer in their Lord?

主的秘密信徒嗎？


He had practically admitted

他幾乎已經承認了


Jesus might have been sent by God!

耶穌可能是上帝派來的！


Many in the Council Hall were murmuring and nodding in agreement --

理事會大廳裡許多人低聲交談，點頭表示贊同——


could they also

他們也可以


be on the verge of faith?

處於信仰的邊緣嗎？


💠



Although irreversibly affronted by the Apostles' bold words,

雖然他永遠無法原諒使徒們那些大膽的言論，


Caiaphas sensed the wind of opinion shifting

該亞法感覺到了輿論的轉變之風


toward Gamaliel's completely reasonable counsel --

朝著迦瑪列完全合理的建議——


and chose to go with the flow.

並選擇隨波逐流。


The Chief Priest cleared his throat.

祭司長清了清嗓子。


"In view of our esteemed brother Gamaliel's wise advice,

「鑑於我們尊敬的兄弟迦瑪列的明智建議，


I recommend we have these men flogged,

我建議我們鞭打這些人，


command them again not to teach in the name of the false Messiah, and --"

再次命令他們不要以假彌賽亞的名義教導，並且——」


he continued reluctantly,

他不情願地繼續說，


"release them."

「釋放他們。」


💠



Although for the moment it was expedient

雖然目前它是權宜之計


to go along with the Council's majority opinion,

同意理事會的多數意見，


the Chief Priest was not in the least convinced by Gamaliel's argument.

祭司長絲毫沒有被迦瑪列的論點說服。


This troublesome movement defied all norms! --

這個麻煩的運動違背了所有規範！--


and threatened to derail everything they had worked so hard to establish

並有可能毀掉他們辛苦建立的一切


under the Roman Empire.

在羅馬帝國統治下。


Caiaphas had no doubt

該亞法毫不懷疑


they would be seeing these men again.

他們會再次見到這些人。


And when they did,

當這種情況發生時，


he would do everything in his power

他會盡他所能


to ensure their swift demise

為了確保他們迅速消亡


and eradicate this troublesome movement.

並根除這場麻煩的運動。


💠

37 - Released

釋放

As Peter, John and the other Apostles headed back to their place in Jerusalem,

當彼得、約翰和其他使徒還在返回耶路撒冷住所的路上，


their backs bruised and egos crushed

他們的背部受傷，自尊心被擊碎


by the flogging they'd endured before the Ruling Council,

透過他們在統治理事會面前忍受的鞭打，


the Holy Spirit spoke the words of the Master

聖靈講了大師的話


gently yet clearly

溫柔而清晰


into their hearts.

進入他們的心。


💠



"If the world hates you,

「世界若恨你們，


keep in mind: it hated Me first.

你們該知道它先恨我。


If you belonged to the world,

你們若屬世界，


it would love you as its own.

世界會像愛自己的孩子一樣愛你。


But you do not belong to the world, for i've chosen you out of the world;

可是你們不屬世界，因為我從世界中揀選了你們出來；


this is why the world hates you.

這就是世界討厭你的原因。


Remember the word i spoke to you:

要記得我從前對你們所說的話：


'No servant is greater than his master.'

『奴僕不可能大過主人。』


If they persecuted Me,

如果他們迫害我，


they will persecute you also."

他們也會迫害你。」


💠



This is how they treated our Lord, realized John, his heart filling instantly with an unfathomable joy.

這就是他們對待我們的主的方式，約翰意識到，他的心立刻充滿了深不可測的喜悅。


As He is, so also are we

耶穌如何，我們也如何


in this world!

在這世上！


A wide grin began to spread across his face.

他臉上露出了燦爛的笑容。


💠



At that moment, Peter turned to him and the others,

那一刻，彼得轉向他和其他人，


and spoke the words

並說出了這些話


the Holy Spirit was bringing alive in his heart:

聖靈使這些話語在他心中活了過來：


"Blessed are you, when they criticize you harshly,

「你有福了，當他們嚴厲地批評你時，


persecute and slander you,

迫害你們、揑造各樣壞話毀謗你們，


because of Me.

因我。


Rejoice and be glad: you have a great reward in Heaven! --

應當歡喜快樂，因為你們在天上的賞賜是大的！——


for in the same way they persecuted

因為人也以同樣的方式迫害


the prophets who were before you!"

在你們以前的先知！」


💠



In spite of their tiredness, hunger, bruises and humiliation, the Apostles now sang --

儘管他們疲憊、飢餓、瘀傷和羞辱，使徒們現在唱著——


some even danced! --

有些人甚至跳舞了！——


all the way home.

一路走回家。


They had been counted worthy to suffer

他們被認為是值得受苦的


for the Name of their crucified Lord:

因為了他們被釘十字架的主的名：


Jesus.

耶穌。


💠



The very next day -- and every day after that --

第二天——以及之後的每一天——


they were back in the Temple --

他們回到了聖殿——


and in the house --

並且在房子裡——


teaching the hungry

教飢餓的人


about Jesus,

關於耶穌，


their risen Messiah.

他是他們復活的彌賽亞。


💠



Based on Acts 5:40-42; John 15:18-21; 1 John 4:17; Matthew 5:11-12.

基於使徒行傳5:40-42；約翰福音15:18-21；約翰一書4:17；馬太福音5:11-12。

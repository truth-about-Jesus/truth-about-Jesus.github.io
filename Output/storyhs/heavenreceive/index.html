47 - Heavenly reception

天堂招待會

As Stephen's spirit rose from his broken, lifeless body,

當斯蒂芬的精神從他破碎、毫無生氣的身體中升起時，


he noticed,

他注意到了一個人，


standing a distance behind his attackers,

站在攻擊他的人群後方一段距離，


a young man

一個年輕人


at whose feet the Council members had laid their cloaks

理事會人們把他們的斗篷放在他的腳邊


for safekeeping.

為了安全保管。


Stephen wasn't sure who the youth was, but --

史蒂芬不確定那個年輕人是誰，但是——


somehow,

不知怎的，


inexplicably,

莫名其妙地，


his heart went out to him.

他的心向他伸出了心。


💠



When he arrived in Heaven, to great fanfare and rejoicing,

當他到達天堂時，天使們大張旗鼓地歡欣鼓舞，


he was conducted before the great Throne, where Jesus was still standing,

他被引導到大寶座前，耶穌仍然站在那裡，


waiting to receive him.

等待迎接他。


Before Stephen could bow low before His Lord,

史蒂芬還來不及向他的主俯伏敬拜，


Jesus Himself approached and hugged him affectionately, saying as He did,

耶穌親自走近，深情地擁抱佢，話：


"Welcome home, My beloved child."

「歡迎回家，我親愛的孩子。」


💠



All the pain and sadness of being hated, falsely accused, condemned, and stoned to death

被憎恨、被誣告、譴責和被石頭砸死的所有痛苦和悲傷


dropped away from Stephen's heart...

離開史蒂芬的心......


vanishing

消失


into

於


eternity.

永恆之中。


💠



Then he heard the Father's voice:

然後他聽到了天父的聲音：


"Well done, good and faithful servant!

「幹得好，善良而忠實的僕人！


You have been faithful in a little;

你在小事上忠心耿耿；


I will set you over much."

我會委以你重任。」


💠



As tears of joy washed over his face,

當喜悅的淚水從他臉上流下來時，


Stephen now bowed low before the great Throne,

史蒂芬在王座前深深鞠躬，


worshipping the One Whose suffering

敬拜那位所主


far outweighed his own.

他的受苦難遠超越自己苦難的。


Waves of glory, peace and joy washed over and through him,

無數的榮耀、和平與喜悅的浪潮沖刷著他，流淌在他的心中，


and he found

並且他發現了


he could truly say:

他真的可以說：


it had all been worthwhile.

這一切都是值得的。


💠



Then he remembered the young fellow he had seen,

然後他想起了他注意到的那個年輕人，


and looked up.

並抬頭看了看。


"My Lord and King, may i --

「我的主和國王，我可以——


make a request?"

提出請求？」


💠



Based on Acts 7:58-60; Matthew 25:14-21.

基於使徒行傳7:58-60；馬太福音25:14-21。

46 - Stoning

石刑

As soon as the words were out of Stephen's mouth,

史蒂芬一說出這些話，


the Council members, already trembling with barely suppressed rage,

理事會早已怒火中燒，全身顫抖，幾乎無法抑制，


howled with horror,

發出厭惡的嚎叫，


covered their ears,

捂住他們的耳朵，


and rushed toward him.

並衝向他。


💠



Once they had dragged Stephen out of the city,

一旦他們把史蒂芬拖出城市，


the maddened witnesses

瘋狂震怒的證人


to his inexcusable cry of joy

聽見了他那不可饒恕的喜樂之聲


seized stones

奪取了石頭


to hurl at the detestable blasphemer.

向可恨的褻瀆者投擲。


In Heaven, the Father shook His Head sadly.

在天堂，天父悲傷地搖了搖頭。


Tears glistened in Jesus' eyes, as He relived His own torment

耶穌眼中閃爍著淚光，他想起了自己曾經遭受的苦難


at the hands of those

在那些人的手中


claiming to represent His Father.

他們聲稱代表他的父親。


💠



The Holy Spirit, who had been flowing through Stephen

聖靈，一直流經斯蒂芬


throughout his trial,

在他的整個審判過程中，


filling him with needed faith and courage,

讓他充滿必要的信心和勇氣，


now rose majestically within,

現在在內心壯麗地升起，


overflowing him with such comfort, peace, strength,

讓他充滿這樣的安慰、和平、力量，


kindness, mercy and compassion,

善良、憐憫和同情心，


that he did not resist his murderers.

他沒有反抗兇手。


Instead, knowing his God-appointed time was near,

相反，他知道他被上帝指定的時間臨近了，


Stephen cried out,

史蒂芬大聲喊道，


"Lord Jesus,

「主耶穌，


receive my spirit!"

接受我的靈魂！」


He knelt down in prayer,

他跪下祈禱，


as a barrage of rocks bruised and battered his body and head,

當一連串的岩石挫傷和毆打他的身體和頭部時，


and cried out, one last time:

最後一次喊道：


"Lord,

「主啊，


do not hold this sin against them!"

不要把這種罪行歸咎於他們！」


💠



Based on Acts 7:54-60.

基於使徒行傳7:54-60。

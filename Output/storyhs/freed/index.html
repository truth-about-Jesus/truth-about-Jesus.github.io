29 - Freed

釋放的

John looked around.

約翰環顧四周。


The angel had disappeared, yet he -- and all the Apostles -- were still standing together in the darkened street!

天使已經消失了，但他——和所有使徒——仍然一起站在黑暗的街道上！


They stared at one another.

他們互相凝視著。


Were they dreaming?

他們在做夢嗎？


Or were they really free?

還是他們真的是自由的？


Either way, they had better obey.

無論哪種方式，他們最好服從。


They headed off toward the Temple

他們向聖殿走去


as the sky began to lighten in the east.

當東方的天空開始變亮時。


💠



Caiaphas surveyed the Council Hall with satisfaction.

該亞法滿意地觀了議會大廳。


All were present and accounted for -- not hastily, but at his leisure.

所有人都到齊了，人數都清點完畢──不是匆忙，而是在他有條不紊的情況下。


The Chief Priest had enjoyed the respite afforded by keeping the false Messiah's main followers in jail.

大祭司透過將假彌賽亞的主要追隨者關在監獄裡而享受了喘息。


However, it was time to deal with them sternly,

然而，是時候嚴厲對待他們了，


and end this atrocious movement,

結束這場可怕的運動，


once and for all.

一勞永逸。


He turned to the bailiff.

他轉向法庭監守。


"Bring in the accused."

「把被告們帶進來。」


💠



The bailiff nodded to the guards, who saluted smartly and exited the Council Hall.

法庭監守向警衛點了點頭，衛兵們敬了個漂亮的軍禮，然後離開了議會大廳。


All waited patiently, some chatting quietly to their neighbour, shifting in their seats, arranging long robes.

所有人都耐心地等待著，有些人靜靜地和鄰居聊天，在座位上挪動一下，整理長袍。


Hmmm...

誒......


The wait seemed to be taking longer than usual...!

等待的時間似乎比平時要長......！


💠



At last, the guards returned emptyhanded, and one of them went straight to the bailiff and whispered in his ear.

最後，警衛們空手而歸，其中一個直接走到法庭監守那裡，在他耳邊低聲說。


The bailiff's eyes widened.

法庭監守睜大了眼睛。


"Are you sure?!"

「你確定嗎？！」

35 - Gamaliel

迦瑪列

Just as Nicodemus and Joseph began to despair for the lives of their Lord's Apostles...

就像尼哥底母和約瑟夫開始對他們主使徒的生命感到絕望一樣......


one of the Council,

理事會的一名成員，


a well-known and highly respected teacher of the Law

一位著名且備受尊敬的法律教師


named Gamaliel,

名為迦瑪列，


who had remained seated while the others were expressing their outrage,

當其他人表達他們的憤怒時，他仍然坐著，


rose calmly from his place.

平靜地從他的地方站起來。


A hush began to fall over the Council Hall,

理事會大廳開始陷入寂安靜，


as Gamaliel signalled the Temple guards to remove the Apostles from the room.

當迦瑪列向聖殿衛士發出訊號，他們把使徒們帶出房間。


Then,

然後，


when all was quiet,

當一切人都安靜的時候，


he spoke.

他說話了。


💠



"Men, Israelites, take a moment

「男人，以色列人，花點時間


and think carefully

並仔細思考


about what you ought to do with these men."

關於你應該對這些人做什麼。」


A few who were standing

有幾個站著的人


began to resume their seats.

決定坐下。


Gamaliel continued:

迦瑪列繼續說道：


"We've dealt with false messiahs before: remember Theudas?

「我們以前處理過虛假的彌賽亞：還記得特烏達斯嗎？


He made great claims for himself, and about four hundred followed him.

他對自己吹噓得非常厲害，進而大約有四百人跟隨他。


But when he was killed, all his followers were dispersed

但當他被殺時，他所有的追隨者都分散了


and the movement came to nothing."

他的運動最終化為烏有。」


More sat now, remembering.

更多人坐了下來，記得。


"And after him, Judas from Galilee rose to prominence, in the days of the head tax enrollment; he attracted many followers!"

「在他之後，來自加利利的猶大在頭稅登記的日子裡嶄露頭角；他吸引了許多追隨者！」


He looked around.

他環顧四周。


"But when he died, all of his followers were scattered, too."

「但當他去世時，他所有的追隨者也散了。」


Heads nodded in sage agreement now, as nearly all were back in their seats.

現在大多數人都明智地點頭表示贊同，在幾乎所有人都回到了座位上。


💠



"So now, I say to you:

「所以現在，我對你說：


Desist from these men, and leave them be.

遠離這些人，讓他們一個人待著。


For if this movement is from man,

因為如果這個運動來自人類，


it will come to an end and disappear."

它將結束並消失。」


Many of the Council felt calmer now -- even Caiaphas! --

許多理事會成員現在感覺更平靜了——即使是該亞法！——


at the thought of the false Messiah's movement

一想到假彌賽亞的運動


dissolving on its own.

自行溶解。


"But if it is from God, you will not be able to stop it.

「但如果它來自上帝，你將無法阻止它。


You will only find yourselves

你們只會發現自己


fighting

戰鬥


against

反抗


God."

上帝。」

基於使徒行傳5:33-39。

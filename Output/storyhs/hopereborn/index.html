19 - Hope reborn

希望重生

"Rise up...

「站起來......


and walk!"

並且走路！」


As the lame man stared at the Apostle's outstretched hand,

當瘸子盯著使徒伸出的手時，


hope,

希望，


reborn,

重生，


overflowed his heart...

填滿了他的心，以至於它溢位來......


dispelling every doubt.

消除每一個疑慮。


💠



With surreal faith, the beggar

帶著超現實的信仰，那個乞丐


seized Peter's hand

抓住彼得的手


and pulled himself up to standing --

並把自己拉起來站著——


for the very first time! --

第一次！--


his misshapen feet and ankles following suit! --

他七扭八歪的腳和腳踝也跟著！--


unthinkably,

難以想象的，


miraculously,

奇蹟般地，


obediently

順從地


becoming healthy and strong.

變得健康和強壯。


💠



He had never stood before -- except in dreams.

他以前從未站過——除了在夢中。


But now he was.

但現在他是。


Standing!

站著！


The former lame man stared at his perfectly formed feet and ankles --

這位前瘸子盯著他完美的腳和腳踝——


they were so beautiful!

他們太漂亮了！


He pinched himself, then slapped his face a few times, as Peter and John looked on with wide grins.

他掐了自己一下，然後又打了自己幾巴掌，而彼得和約翰則咧嘴大笑著看著。


At last, Azariah was certain:

最後，亞撒利雅確信了：


This...

這個...


was real.

是真的。


💠



Based on Acts 3:6-8.

基於使徒行傳3:6-8。

1 - Beginnings

開始

Esau's Mom and Dad were definitely chosen people.

以掃的爸爸媽媽絕對是被選中的人。


His Dad, Isaac, was the only son born to Grandpa Abraham

他的父親艾薩克是亞伯拉罕爺爺唯一的兒子


through his first wife, Grandma Sarah.

透過他的第一任妻子，莎拉奶奶。


Although Esau's Dad had many half-brothers --

儘管以掃的爸爸有很多同父異母的兄弟——


through Grandpa's concubine

透過爺爺的妾


and second wife --

和他的第二任妻子——


only Isaac had inherited everything Grandpa Abraham had owned.

只有艾薩克繼承了亞伯拉罕爺爺所擁有的一切。


And only Isaac had had his marriage

只有艾薩克的婚姻


specially arranged for him by God.

是上帝專門為祂安排的。


💠



Esau had heard the story a thousand times,

以掃已經聽過這個故事一千遍了，


as his Mom and Dad liked to recount it at dinner often enough:

正如他的爸爸媽媽經常喜歡在晚餐時講述的那樣：


Grandpa Abraham had sent his most trusted servant back home

亞伯拉罕爺爺把他最信任的僕人送回了家


to the city of Nahor in Mesopotamia

前往美索不達米亞的拿鶴城


to look for a wife for his Dad.

為他爸爸找一個妻子。


His Mom, who had been just a girl back then,

他的媽媽，那時她只是一個女孩，


had done exactly the things Grandpa's servant had prayed for:

她完全做到了爺爺的僕人所祈禱的事情：


not only had she given him a drink,

她不僅給了他水喝，


but she'd volunteered to water his ten camels as well!

但她也自願給他的十隻駱駝水喝！


Good thing Mom is such a go-getter, Esau grinned to himself...

幸好媽媽是個進取，以掃暗自得意地笑了…


just like me.

就像我一樣。


💠



His Dad, Isaac, was a quiet man --

他的父親艾薩克是個安靜的人——


kind of like his twin brother, Jacob,

有點像他的雙胞胎兄弟雅各，


who never really did much.

他從未真正做過什。


Dad had gone out to the field alone to meditate,

爸爸一個人去田野冥想，


the day Grandpa's servant arrived with Mom.

爺爺的僕人和媽媽一起到達的那天。


The servant told Dad of his adventures in Mesopotamia,

僕人告訴爸爸他在美索不達米亞的冒險經歷，


Dad took Mom into his tent,

爸爸帶媽媽進了他的帳篷，


and the rest...

以及其他一切…


was history.

是歷史。

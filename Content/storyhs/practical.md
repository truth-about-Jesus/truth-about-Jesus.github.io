Meanwhile, what was being done 

with all the money the believers were bringing to the Apostles?

They were not using it to build buildings

or pay salaries.

Instead, the Apostles were distributing the money daily 

to the needy among them,

particularly to widows who had no family 

or other support.

💠

After a time, however, certain of the believers complained that their widows were not receiving 

desperately needed funds,

while others were,

and it soon became clear that better organization was needed

to ensure fairness. 

By now there were many thousands of believers, and

despite their best efforts, the Apostles were struggling to keep up with the daily distribution of money to the poor,

in addition 

to obeying their Master's calling. 

So...

the Holy Spirit 

called a meeting.

💠

"i nominate Stephen!"

All of the believers were gathered together in Solomon's Colonnade 

in the courtyard of the Temple, 

where they met regularly to listen to the teaching of the Apostles.

But today's meeting was different.

Today, they were choosing seven men 

from among them

to take care of the donated funds

and free up the Apostles to spend their time

in prayer 

and teaching the Word.

💠

In the end, Stephen and six others -- 

capable, honest, trustworthy men, filled with the Holy Spirit, wisdom and faith -- 

were chosen.

The Apostles placed their hands on them, 

prayed for them

and commissioned them

to take care of the needy.

💠

Based on Acts 4:32-37; Acts 6:1-7.

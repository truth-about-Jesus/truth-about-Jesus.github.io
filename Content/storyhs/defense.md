The Father nudged Jesus.

"It's nearly time."

Both were seated together on the Father's large throne in Heaven, 

Jesus at His Father's right hand, as always,

watching, listening, and strengthening their child, Stephen,

as he stood on trial

before the Jewish Ruling Council. 

💠

Through Stephen, the Holy Spirit had been recapping, in brief, the stories of 

Abraham, Isaac, Jacob, Joseph, 

Moses, Joshua, 

David and Solomon,

tracing the history of Israel 

down through the generations,

highlighting in particular 

Israel's rebellion against Moses, God's chosen leader,

and against God Himself,

as the Council listened.

Even though Stephen's life was on the line, 

the Holy Spirit did not bother to respond to any 

of the false accusations levelled against him.

💠

At last, it was time.

The Holy Spirit spoke the words he knew 

would infuriate the Council members --

which nonetheless must be spoken.

"You stiff-necked people, uncircumcised in hearts and ears! 

You always resist 

the Holy Spirit, 

just as your fathers did!

Which of the prophets 

didn't your fathers persecute? 

They killed those 

who foretold the coming of the Righteous One, 

and now you 

have betrayed and murdered Him!

You received the Law from angels, 

and have not kept it!"

💠

Based on Acts 6:9-15; Acts 7:1-53; Matthew 10:17-20.

The Father looked,

父親眼，

\

and instantly, Gabriel,

立刻，加百列，

\

His Chief Messenger angel 

祂的首席信使天使

\

stood before the big Throne,

站在大寶座前，

\

respectful and alert.

尊重和願意。

\

"It's time for you to visit Zechariah the priest, in the Temple, Mary at home in Nazareth, and her fiancé, Joseph, the carpenter.

「是時候讓你去拜訪聖殿的祭司撒迦利亞，在拿撒勒的馬利亞，以及她的未婚夫，木匠約瑟夫。

\

"For the carpenter, be sure to visit in a dream, 

「對於木匠來說，一定要在夢中拜訪，

\

*not* while he's awake.

不是在他醒著的時候。

\

It will be best for him that way."

那樣對他來說是最好的。」

\

Gabriel saluted smartly.

加百列巧妙地敬禮。

\

"Anything else, My Lord?"

「還有別的事嗎，大人？」

\

The Father smiled at His faithful servant.

父親對祂忠實的僕人笑了笑。

\

"When you get back,

「當你回來的時候，

\

get the choir -- the *big* one -- ready!"

讓合唱團——大合唱團——做好準備！」

\

<center>💠</center>

\
\

Gabriel took care of his assignment quickly.

加百列很快就完成了他的任務。

\

Although time on Earth passes slowly --

儘管地球上的時間過得很慢——

\

a full *six* earth months separating the visits to Zechariah 

從他第一次拜訪撒迦利亞

\

and Mary --

到第二次拜訪馬利亞，中間隔了整整六個月——

\

Gabriel, existing as he did in eternity,

加百列，就像他在永恆中一樣存在，

\

easily completed all three visits 

輕鬆完成了所有三次訪問

\

in less than *one* heavenly day.

不到一個天堂一天。

\

<center>💠</center>

\
\

When he arrived back home in Heaven, the Chief Messenger angel stood before the great Throne to give his report.

當他回到天堂的家時，首席使者天使站在偉大的寶座前報告。

\

"How did things go?"

「事情怎麼樣了？」

\

"All Your messages have been delivered. 

「你的所有資訊都已送達。

\

Mary has been to visit Elizabeth 

馬利亞去拜訪了伊莉莎白

\

and both are aware of the other's pregnancy.

兩人都知道對方懷孕了。

\

And the carpenter and Mary have tied the knot, so to speak -- 

木匠和馬利亞結婚了——

\

not physically, of course."

當然，不是指物理意義上的。」

\

"And Zechariah?"

「撒迦利亞呢？」

\

Gabriel sighed, just a little, and tried not to roll his eyes.

加百列嘆了口氣，只是一點點，儘量不翻白眼。

\

"I had to strike him dumb for a little, to help him believe."

「我不得不暫時剝奪他的說話能力，幫助他相信這一點。」

\

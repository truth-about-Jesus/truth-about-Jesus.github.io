After what seemed an eternity 

在看似永恆之後

\

to both Rebekah and Isaac --

給麗貝卡和艾薩克——

\

although it really wasn't terribly long, 

雖然它真的不是很長，

\

the midwife knew --

助產士知道——

\

Rebekah's deep belly gave a tremendous thrust --

麗貝卡深深的腹部產生了巨大的推動力——

\

and newborn Esau's head popped out

新生兒以掃的頭彈了出來

\

followed quickly by his much smaller body.

緊隨其後的是他更小的身體。

\

<center>💠</center>

\
\

Isaac barely had time to be amazed at how red and hairy his first son was,

艾薩克幾乎沒有時間驚訝於他的第一個兒子有多紅，有多毛，

\

when the second, baby Jacob, 

當第二個，雅各寶寶，

\

holding tightly to Esau's heel,

緊緊抓住以掃的腳後跟，

\

was also born

他也出生了

\

in a rush.

匆匆忙忙。

\

It was almost as if 

這幾乎就像

\

the entire birthing experience 

整個分娩經歷

\

had been a *race* between the two

是兩者之間的一場競賽

\

to see who would be born first!

看看誰會先出生！

\

<center>💠</center>

\
\

*But that's ridiculous,* thought Rebekah to herself many hours later,

但這太荒謬了，幾個小時後，麗貝卡心想，

\

once she had recovered somewhat from the exhausting, alarming experience,

一旦她從疲憊、令人震驚的經歷中恢復過來，

\

as two very different newborns -- 

作為兩個截然不同的新生兒——

\

one hairy -- *almost furry!* --

一個毛茸茸的——簡直就像覆蓋著動物的皮毛！——

\

the other with skin as smooth and hairless as an eel's -- 

另一個皮膚像鰻魚一樣光滑無毛——

\

nestled in her arms.

依偎在她的懷裡。

\

*They're only babies!*

他們只是嬰兒！

\

*They don't know anything about the rights of the firstborn...*

他們對長子的權利一無所知......

\

*do they?*

他們知嗎？

\

"Lord God, why send me this beautiful wife,

「主上帝，為什麼要把這個美麗的妻子送給我，

\

if You don't intend for her to have children?"

如果你不打算讓她生孩子？」

\

Isaac and Rebekah had been married for almost twenty years now --

艾薩克和麗貝卡已經結婚快二十年了——

\

*twenty years! --*

二十年！--

\

and they still didn't have a single child.

他們仍然沒有一個孩子。

\

<center>💠</center>

\
\

Isaac had noticed 

艾薩克已經注意到了

\

Rebekah was beginning to look like she felt sad

麗貝卡開始看起來很悲傷了

\

all of the time now,

現在所有的時間，

\

although she didn't say anything --

雖然她什麼也沒說——

\

*which was in itself unusual for her! --*

這對她來說很不尋常！--

\

so he took up the matter with God.

所以他向上帝處理了這件事。

\

"Please give Rebekah a child, Lord God."

「主上帝，請給麗貝卡一個孩子。」

\

<center>💠</center>

\
\

Isaac knew his *own* mother, Sarah, had not had *him* 

艾薩克知道，他自己的母親莎拉

\

until she was already *ninety* years old -- 

她直到90歲才生下他——

\

*well past child-bearing age! --*

遠遠超過育齡期！--

\

so he knew for certain:

所以他肯定知道：

\

God could *easily* do 

上帝可以輕易做到

\

the same for Rebekah. 

麗貝卡也能經歷同樣的奇蹟。

\

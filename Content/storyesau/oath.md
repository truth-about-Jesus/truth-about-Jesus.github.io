*That tantalizing aroma...*

那誘人的香氣......

\

Esau breathed it in --

以掃吸了一口氣——

\

and instantly, his hunger found a focus.

瞬間，他的飢餓找到了焦點。

\

He burst into Jacob's tent,

他衝進了雅各的帳篷，

\

his eyes rivetted on the red lentil stew 

他的眼睛盯著紅扁豆燉菜

\

that could revitalize 

那可以振興

\

his depleted body.

他憊的身體。

\

"Let me swallow some of that red-*red* --"

「讓我吞下一些紅紅的——」

\

He was almost too hungry to think.

他餓得幾乎無法思考。

\

"I'm *starving!"*

「我快餓死了！」

\

<center>💠</center>

\
\

Jacob studied his older twin carefully.

雅各仔細地打量著他的雙胞胎哥哥。

\

He didn't *look* dangerous at the moment...

他此刻看起來並不危險......

\

"First, sell me your birthright," he responded decisively

「首先，把你當長子所擁有的權利賣給我，」他果斷地回答道

\

after a pause,

停頓後，

\

trembling a little,

然後有點顫抖，

\

as he anticipated Esau's response.

等待以掃的回應。

\

<center>💠</center>

\
\

"I'm about to *die!"* Esau retorted,

「我快要死了！」 以掃反駁道，

\

his entire body focussed on the sumptuous stew 

他全身都專注於豐盛的燉菜

\

simmering over Jacob's fire.

在雅各的火上沸騰。

\

"Who cares about a birthright? 

我不在乎長子所擁有的權利！

\

*Give* me the stew; it's *yours!"*

把燉菜給我；你可以擁有它！」

\

<center>💠</center>

\
\

Jacob hesitated.

雅各布猶豫了一下。

\

This was *too* easy.

這太容易了。

\

Would Esau *remember*... later?

以掃會記得......以後嗎？

\

He needed to be sure.

他需要確定。

\

"Swear an oath to me right now,

「現在就向我發誓，

\

if you're serious."

如果你是認真的。」

\

<center>💠</center>

\
\

Esau rolled his eyes, and impatiently repeated the words his brother was saying,

以掃翻了個白眼，不耐煩地重複著他哥哥說的話，

\

all the while thinking only of 

一直只想著

\

that mouth-wateringly irresistible stew.

那個令人垂涎欲滴的燉菜。

\

"I, Esau, do solemnly swear that i sell Jacob my birthright --

「我，以掃，鄭重發誓，我把作為長子所擁有的權利賣給了雅各——

\

in exchange for this pot of stew."

為了換取這鍋燉菜。」

\

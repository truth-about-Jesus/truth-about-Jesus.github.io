"Two nations are in your womb, 

「兩個國家在你的子宮裡，

\

and two separate peoples arise from your belly.

兩個獨立的民族從你的肚子裡冒出來。

\

One people is stronger than the other; 

一個國家比另一個國家更強大；

\

and the *older* serves the *younger."*

哥哥服侍弟弟。」

\

<center>💠</center>

\
\

Rebekah puzzled over the words she'd received from God.

麗貝卡琢磨她從上帝那裡得到的啟示。

\

*What could they possibly mean?!*

這到底是什麼意思？ ！

\

Could she really become 

她真的能成為嗎

\

the mother 

母親

\

of *two whole nations?!*

兩個完整的國家？！

\

<center>💠</center>

\
\

At least one thing was clear --

至少有一件事是清楚的——

\

although Rebekah still couldn't quite believe it:

儘管麗貝卡仍然無法完全相信：

\

she was going to have... 

她將生下...

\

*twins...?!*

雙胞胎......？！

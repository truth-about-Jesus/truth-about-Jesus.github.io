Esau was relaxing with his two Canaanite wives,

以掃正在和他的兩個迦南妻子一起放鬆，

\

Judith and Basemath,

朱迪思和巴實抹，

\

when his mother Rebekah called him from outside the tent. 

當他的母親麗貝卡從帳篷外叫他時。

\

*"Esau!"*

「以掃！」

\

"Yes, Mother?" he responded respectfully. His wives rolled their eyes at each other.

「媽媽，我在！」 他恭敬地回應。 他的妻子們互相翻了個白眼。

\

"Your father is calling for you."

「你父親在呼喚你。」

\

He got up quickly.

他很快就起床了。

\

Dad was older and couldn't see well anymore,

爸爸年紀大了，已經看不清楚了，

\

so he didn't want to keep him waiting. 

所以他不想讓他等。

\

<center>💠</center>

\
\

"My son?"

「我的兒子？」

\

"I'm here, Dad."

「我在，爸爸。」

\

Isaac's face brightened.

艾薩克的臉亮了起來。

\

Although he couldn't see any longer, he cherished the sound of his older son's voice. 

雖然他再也看不見了，但他很珍惜他大兒子的聲音。

\

Esau was a man's man, a skilled outdoorsman who loved to go hunting, and the wonderful flavour of the venison he brought home 

以掃是個男人，一個熟練的戶外愛好者，喜歡打獵，還有他帶回家的鹿肉的美妙味道

\

had cemented a special bond between father and son.

鞏固了父子之間的特殊紐帶。

\

<center>💠</center>

\
\

Esau waited. His Dad was over a hundred years old now, and sometimes took a moment to gather his thoughts.

以掃等待著。 他的父親現在已經一百多歲了，有時會花點時間整理他的想法。

\

At last, Isaac continued.

最後，艾薩克繼續說道。

\

"I'm old now, and don't know when i will die."

「我現在老了，不知道我什麼時候會死。」

\

Esau began to protest, but his father held up a warning hand, so he stopped, waiting, as his father prepared to speak again.

以掃開始抗議，但他的父親舉起了警告的手，所以他停下來，等待著，因為他的父親準備再次說話。

\

"So, please take your quiver and your bow, and go out hunting, as you love to do!

「所以，請帶上你的箭筒和弓，像你做什事的那樣出去打獵吧！

\

Catch some game, and prepare it for me in that tasty way you know i love.

去獵些野味，然後用我喜歡的美味方式烹調給我。

\

I will eat it,

我會吃它，

\

and my soul will bless you 

我的靈魂會祝福你

\

before i die."

在我死之前。」

\

<center>💠</center>

\
\

Esau stood up straight --

以掃站直了身子——

\

*"Yes, sir!" --*

「是的，先生！」——

\

and headed out immediately.

並立即出發。

\

*Maybe* Jacob had tricked him 

也許雅各騙了他

\

into giving up his birthright --

放棄他的長子所擁有的權利——

\

but he *still* had his father's love,

但他仍然有他父親的愛，

\

and would *soon* have his blessing!

並且很快就會得到他的祝福！

\

"Oh, *man!* I'm gonna *die!"*

「啊！我餓死了！」

\

Esau had come back from hunting --

以掃打獵回來了——

\

his singular skill and favourite pursuit --

他獨特的技能和最喜歡的追求——

\

ravenous.

大飢餓的。

\

The youth had been out chasing a deer, 

這個年輕人一直在追趕一隻鹿，

\

a beautiful buck,

一隻強壯健康的雄鹿，

\

which had led him further away than ever before,

這讓他比以往任何時候都離家更遠，

\

and he'd tired himself out in the chase.

他在追逐中疲憊不堪。

\

He'd caught it, yes, and barely managed to bring it home, but now...

是的，他抓住了它，幾乎不可能，但把它帶回家，

\

he was exhausted.

現在...... 他精疲力竭。

\

<center>💠</center>

\
\

Jacob looked up from the hearty, delicious lentil stew he had been making,

雅各從他一直在做的豐盛美味的扁豆燉菜中抬起頭來，

\

its wonderful aroma wafting through the doorway of his tent, as intended.

正如他打算的那樣，它美妙的香氣從他的帳篷門口飄過。

\

*Had his chance come at last?*

他終於有機會了嗎？

\

He trembled a little as he weighed the consequences.

當他權衡後果時，他有點顫抖。

\

Esau had always been the stronger of the two;

以掃一直比他強壯一些；

\

many times he had beaten his younger twin in a fist fight --

他曾多次在互毆中擊敗他的雙胞胎弟弟——

\

actually, *every* time, 

實際上，每次，

\

if Jacob were honest.

如果雅各是誠實的。

\

Their father had been perturbed by the scene,

他們的父親因看到他們打架而感到不安，

\

but their mother had laughed it off 

但他們的母親笑掉了它

\

as boyish antics,

作為如同男孩般的滑稽舉動，

\

later comforting her younger son 

後來安慰她的小兒子

\

with the story of 

講述這個故事

\

God's words about them:

上帝關於他們的話：

\

"One is stronger than the other; 

「一個民族比另一個民族更強大；

\

and the *older serves* the younger."

哥哥服侍弟弟。」

\

<center>💠</center>

\
\

After numerous fistfights,

經過多次互毆之後，

\

Jacob had come to realize

雅各已經意識到了

\

he would only ever 

他只會

\

fulfill

滿足

\

God's words about him

上帝關於他的話

\

*not* by force --

不是用武力——

\

but some *other* way...

但用另一種更好、更簡單的方式......

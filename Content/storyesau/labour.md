"Ee-*yaaaarghhhhh!"* wailed Rebekah.

「咿啊啊啊！」 麗貝卡哀號。

\

She'd awoken that morning feeling different,

那天早上她醒來時感覺不一樣了，

\

as if, every so often, her belly 

彷彿，每隔一段時間，她的肚子下面

\

was seizing up deep inside.

內部痙攣。

\

Isaac had sent for the midwife,

艾薩克派人去助產士，

\

who'd come immediately.

她立刻就來了。

\

She was here now,

她現在在這裡，

\

handing a clean, damp cloth to Rebekah's nurse,

助產士遞給麗貝卡的護士一塊乾淨的濕布，

\

and instructing her to wipe the perspiration from Rebekah's forehead.

並指示她擦拭麗貝卡額頭上的汗水。

\

<center>💠</center>

\
\

Isaac stood by nervously, wanting very much to go outside

艾薩克緊張地站在一旁，非常想出去

\

away from all of the hullabaloo --

遠離所有的騷動——

\

yet also not wanting to miss out on the birth of his child --

但也不想錯過他孩子的出生——

\

or *children,* if what Rebekah had told him 

或者孩子們，如果麗貝卡告訴他的故事

\

was to be believed!

屬實！

\

<center>💠</center>

\
\

Even though he was all of sixty years old now,

儘管他現在已經六十歲了，

\

he still felt uncertain about being the father of *one* child --

他仍然對做一個孩子的父親感到不確定——

\

let alone *two.*

更不用說兩個了。

\

Yes, he and Rebekah both definitely *wanted* children...

是的，他和麗貝卡肯定都想要孩子......

\

it was just...

這只是......

\

now that parenthood was imminent...

既然即將為人父母......

\

was he --

他是——

\

*ready?*

準備好了嗎？

\

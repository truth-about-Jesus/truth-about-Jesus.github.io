1 - Anticipation

預期

The Father nudged Jesus

天父輕輕推了耶穌


as they sat together on their big Throne in Heaven.

當他們一起坐在天堂的大寶座上時。


"It's nearly

「這幾乎是


Time."

時間。」


💠



Jesus took a deep breath

耶穌深吸了一口氣


and shuddered a little

有點發抖


as He contemplated the strange transformation He was soon to undergo.

當他思考他即將經歷的奇怪轉變時。


He loved being in Heaven

他非常喜歡待在天堂


with His Father!

和他父親一起！


Together, They shared

他們一起分享了


unimaginable Beauty,

難以想象的美麗，


unlimited Power,

無限的力量，


unfathomable Wisdom,

最深邃的智慧，


unconquerable Love.

不可征服的愛。


Almost every moment was filled

幾乎每一刻都充滿了


with exquisite melodies of worship and adoration

帶著精緻的崇拜和崇拜的旋律


provided by luminous, harmonious, powerful angelic beings

由發光、和諧、強大的天使生物提供


eager to do their Lord's bidding.

渴望完成他們主的命令。


💠



How

怎麼


could He leave all this glory, beauty and holiness

他能拋棄所有的榮耀、美麗和聖潔嗎


to go down

下去


to a dirty, doubt-ridden, death-filled

到一個骯髒的、充滿懷疑的、充滿死亡的


world gone wrong --

世界出錯了——


not as the conquering Creator God He truly was,

祂並非以凱旋的造物主形像出現，但這才是祂的真實身分，


but as a tiny, helpless,

但作為一個渺小的、無助的，


impoverished,

貧窮的，


human

人類


infant?!

新生兒？！


💠



He knew what would happen to Him --

他知道他會發生什麼——


His entire life on earth

他在地球上的一生


from beginning to end --

從開始到結束——


for He and His Father had planned it

因為他和他父親已經計劃好了


together.

一起。


It would be the hardest thing He --

這將是他——


or anyone --

或者任何人——


had ever done.

經歷過的最艱難的事。


💠



Based on John 1:1-5,10-14,18; Matthew 1:18-25; Luke 1:30-35; Luke 2:34-35; Isaiah 53:2-12.

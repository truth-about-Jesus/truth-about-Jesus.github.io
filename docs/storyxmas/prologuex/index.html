序言

Nearly everyone has heard the story of the birth of Jesus --

幾乎每個人都聽說過耶穌誕生的故事——


born of a virgin,

處女所生，


heralded by angels,

由天使宣布，


worshipped by shepherds,

被牧羊人崇拜，


receiving rich gifts from wise men from the east,

收到來自東方智者的豐富禮物，


His first home a stable,

他的第一個家是馬廄，


His first crib a feeding trough --

他的第一個嬰兒床是一個餵食槽——


or manger.

或者馬槽。


💠



But --

但是——


what was happening...

發生了什麼......


behind the scenes?

幕後？


💠



Based on Matthew 1:18-25; Matthew 2:1-18; Luke 1:5-80; Luke 2:1-39; John 1:1-18.

基於馬太福音1:18-25；馬太福音2:1-18；路加福音1:5-80；路加福音2:1-39；約翰福音1:1-18。

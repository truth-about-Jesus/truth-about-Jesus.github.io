4 - Incarnation

化身

Jesus was falling toward the darkened Earth

耶穌正向黑暗的地球墜落


and shrinking

並且縮小


at the same time.

同時。


It was a bizarre sensation

那是一種奇怪的感覺


to become less

變得更少


than He really was,

比他真正的，


but by faith

但憑著信心


He chose

他選擇了


to relax

放鬆


and receive.

並受。


With His Father's help

在他父親的幫助下


He would begin --

他將開始——


and complete --

並完成——


the most unthinkable transformation

最不可思議的轉變


anyone anywhere

任何地方的任何人


had ever undertaken.

曾經承擔過。


💠



Father!

父親！


i can't see anything!

我什麼都看不見！


Jesus could no longer speak aloud --

耶穌再也無法大聲說話了——


having divested Himself of His Godlike qualities --

祂捨棄了自己神一般的能力——


but He could still sense His Father's presence,

但他仍然能感覺到他父親的存在，


even now.

即使是現在。


Where am i?!

我在哪裡？！


Relax, My Son, came the instant reassurance.

放鬆，我的兒子，瞬間就放心了。


You're in Your mother's womb.

你在你母親的子宮裡。


Ohh...

哦......


After a while,

過了一會兒，


Jesus realized:

耶穌意識到：


it was oddly comforting in here...!

這裡出奇地讓人感到舒適……！


💠



Based on John 1:1-3,14-15,18; Luke 1:26-35; Matthew 1:18-25.

基於約翰福音1:1-3,14-15,18；路加福音1:26-35；馬太福音1:18-25。

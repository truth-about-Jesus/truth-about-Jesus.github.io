3 - Wrestling match

摔跤比賽

"What is going on in there?!" wondered Rebekah to herself.

「裡面到底發生什麼事？！」麗貝卡心想。


She had been overjoyed to discover that,

她欣喜若狂地發現，


at long last,

終於，


she was pregnant

她懷孕了


and would soon give birth to the child

並且很快就會生下


she and Isaac had so greatly desired

她和艾薩克非常渴望的孩子


for nearly twenty years now.

近二十年來。


💠



But she'd never been pregnant before, so

但她以前從未懷孕過，所以


she wasn't sure, but...

她不確定，但是......


was her belly supposed to feel like a wrestling match?!

她的肚子應該感覺像一場摔跤比賽嗎？！


💠



At last, she decided:

最後，她決定了：


"I don't know what is happening,

「我不知道發生什麼，


but the God Who brought me and my husband together,

但把我和我丈夫帶到一起的上帝，


Who answered my husband's prayer to give me a child --

祂回應了我丈夫的祈禱，讓我生一個孩子——


surely He can tell me."

我相信祂能夠告訴我答案。」


💠



Based on Genesis 24; Genesis 25:20-22.

基於創世記24；創世記25:20-22。

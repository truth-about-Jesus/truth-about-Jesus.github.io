9 - One down...

搞定一個...

As Jacob cleared up after his brother Esau's meal,

當雅各在他哥哥以掃吃完飯後清理時，


he reflected again on the prophecy

他再次反思了預言


his Mom had shared with him

他媽媽和他分享了


so many times...

這麼多次......


the one where Esau would one day serve him, the younger twin!

預言說，以掃將服侍他的弟弟！


💠



He had now managed to obtain the birthright --

他現在已經獲得了與生俱來的權利——


the rights of the firstborn son --

長子所擁有的權利——


from his older brother.

從他的哥哥。


He trusted Mom and Dad would respect the oath Esau had sworn --

他相信爸爸媽媽會尊重以掃的誓言——


however unusual!

無論多麼不尋常！


They were both sticklers for that sort of thing.

他們倆都非常重視誓言。


This meant Jacob now stood to inherit a double portion -- or two-thirds -- of his father's property --

這意味著雅各現在將繼承他父親財產的兩倍份額——或三分之二——


even one-third of which was a very sizeable inheritance!

甚至其中三分之一是一筆非常可觀的遺產！


💠



Although he had no idea how he could ever get it,

雖然他完全不知道自己該如何才能得到它，


there was still one thing Jacob needed

雅各還需要一樣東西


to gain full ascendancy over his older brother:

為了完全凌駕於他的哥哥之上：


their father's blessing.

他們父親的祝福。


💠



Based on Genesis 25:21-34; Genesis 26:12-14.

基於創世記25:21-34；創世記26:12-14。

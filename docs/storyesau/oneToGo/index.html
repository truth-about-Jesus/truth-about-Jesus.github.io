11 - One to go

還剩一個

As soon as Esau had gone out, Rebekah,

他們的大兒子剛一出門，麗貝卡，


who had quite naturally been present during the entire conversation,

在整個談話中，她很自然地在場，


excused herself

她請求離開


and hurried to find her younger son, Jacob.

並急忙去找她的小兒子雅各。


He would need to receive his father's blessing

他需要得到他父親的祝福


if he were to fulfill God's words!

如果他要履行上帝對他的預言的話！


💠



"But, Mom!" Jacob protested.

「但是，媽媽！」 雅各布抗議道。


Not only was he unsettled by the thought of deceiving his father,

他不僅對欺騙父親的想法感到不安，


but --

但是——


he wasn't convinced the deception would succeed.

他不相信欺騙會成功。


His brother Esau was very hairy

他的兄弟以掃非常多毛


while he, Jacob, had smooth skin.

而他，雅各，面板光滑。


"What if Dad wants to touch me? Then he'll know it's me

「如果爸爸想摸我怎麼辦？ 然後他就會知道是我


and i'll look like a deceiver -- or worse!

我會看起來像個騙子——或者更糟！


Then maybe

然後也許


instead of being blessed --

而不是被祝福——


i'll be blamed!"

我會被指責的！」


Jacob shuddered at the thought

一想到這個，雅各就打了個寒顫


of his gentle father's disappointment.

他溫柔的父親的失望。


💠



"I will take the blame, my son,"

「我會承擔責任的，我的兒子，」


said Rebekah reassuringly.

麗貝卡安慰了他。


"Just do what i ask:

「就做我要求的：


bring me two healthy kids from the flock of goats

從山羊群中給我帶來兩個健康的仔羊


and i'll make some delicious food for your father --

我會給你父親做一些美味的食物——


just the way he loves."

他很喜歡的那種。」


💠

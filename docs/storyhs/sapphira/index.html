40 - Sapphira

薩菲拉

Three hours later, after the young men had removed her husband's body,

三小時後，在那些年輕男人取出她丈夫的屍體後，


Sapphira arrived,

薩菲拉到了，


unaware

不知道的


of her newfound

她剛剛的


widowhood.

喪偶。


"Tell me, Sapphira," said the Holy Spirit through Peter gently,

「告訴我，薩菲拉，」聖靈透過彼得溫柔地說，


"this money which your husband brought us this morning --

「你丈夫今天早上給我們帶來的這筆錢——


was it the full amount you received from the sale of your field?"

這是你從出售你的田地中獲得的全部金額嗎？」


"Yes, of course it was," she replied quickly, with a big smile, wondering why the shekels were still lying there.

「是的，當然是這樣，」她快速地回答，帶著燦爛的笑容，想知道為什麼錢仍然躺在那裡。


Surely they were grateful to have received such a generous gift!

當然，他們很感激收到如此慷慨的禮物！


"The full amount?" Peter pressed.

「全額？」 彼得追問。


Why did he keep asking? And where was Ananias?

他為什麼一直問？ 阿納尼亞斯在哪裡？


"It was the total sum we received from the sale of our field," she insisted. "We sold it to our neighbour, Abel."

「這是我們從出售田地中獲得的總金額，」她堅持說。 「我們把它賣給了我們的鄰居亞伯。」


Foolish Abel, she smirked inwardly. I still can't believe he gave us such a ridiculous price!

愚蠢的亞伯，她內心咧嘴一笑。 我仍然不敢相信他給了我們如此荒謬的價格！


💠



"Since you and your husband have agreed together

「既然你和你丈夫已經同意了


as equals

作為平等夥伴


to test the Spirit of the Lord," replied the Holy Spirit

考驗主的靈，」聖靈回答道


sorrowfully through Peter,

透過彼得悲傷地，


"Look!"

「看！」


Sapphira looked up and saw some of the young men entering the room,

薩菲拉抬頭一看，看到一些年輕男人走進了房間，


their eyes widening

他們睜大了眼睛


as they saw her smiling at them.

當他們看到她對他們微笑時。


"The feet of those who buried your husband are at the door,

「埋葬你丈夫的人的腳在門口，


and they will carry you out."

他們會把你抬出去。」


She collapsed

她摔倒了


in a heap.

在地上。

41 - Reflection

思慮

Some time after the young men had carried out Sapphira's lifeless body,

在年輕人帶走薩菲拉毫無生氣的身體一段時間後，


Peter contemplated the day's shocking events.

彼得思考了當天令人震驚的事件。


Yes, it had been

是的，確實如此


a serious matter

一件嚴肅的事情


that the two had conspired to lie

兩人密謀撒謊


to the Holy Spirit! --

給聖靈！--


but

但是


death?!

處決？！


Everyone present

每個人都在場


and all who'd heard

以及所有聽過的人


had been terrified.

被嚇壞了。


💠



Peter had himself been taken by surprise

彼得自己也大吃一驚


when Ananias had collapsed and died.

當阿納尼亞斯倒下並去世時。


Of course, he had then expected Sapphira to suffer a similar fate,

當然，他當時預計薩菲拉會遭受類似的命運，


once it was established

一旦它建立起來


the two were equally in agreement

兩人同樣一致


to deceive.

欺騙。


💠



After a while, he remembered what Jesus had said

過了一會兒，他想起了耶穌說過的話


when the Pharisees had been scoffing at Him and loudly attributing

當法利賽人嘲笑他並大聲歸因於


to demonic activity! --

惡魔活動！--


the wonderful healing miracles given by God:

上帝賜予的奇妙的治癒奇蹟：


"He that is not with Me

「誰不和我在一起的人


is against Me;

反對我；


and he that does not gather with Me

以及不與我收集的人


actively scatters.

積極撒。


Whoever speaks a word against the Son of Man

誰說一句話反對人子


will be forgiven:

將被原諒：


but whoever speaks against the Holy Spirit

但誰說出反對聖靈


will not be forgiven,

不會被原諒，


neither in this world,

在這個世界上也沒有，


nor in the world to come."

也不是在來世。」


💠



Based on Acts 5:1-11; Matthew 12:22-32.

基於使徒行傳5:1-11；馬太福音12:22-32。

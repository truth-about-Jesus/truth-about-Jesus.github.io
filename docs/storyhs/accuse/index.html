43 - Accused

指控

"Stephen has spoken blasphemy against Moses!"

「史蒂芬對摩西說了褻瀆！」


"... and God!"

「......還有上帝！」


Stephen was startled.

史蒂芬被嚇了一跳。


Where was this coming from?

這是從哪裡來的？


He didn't even know these men who were accusing him.

他甚至不認識這些指責他的人。


Why were they saying such terrible things about him?

他們為什麼說那些關於他的可怕話？


Yet even now, the Holy Spirit quieted his heart.

然而，即使是現在，聖靈也讓他的心平靜下來。


He was ready to face false accusation, suffering --

他準備面對虛假指控，痛苦——


even death! --

甚至死亡！--


for the sake of his Lord,

為了他的主，


Jesus the Messiah.

彌賽亞耶穌。


💠



Only yesterday, in the course of Stephen's duties

就在昨天，在史蒂芬的職責過程中


distributing funds to the needy,

向有需要的人分配資金，


the Holy Spirit had been doing wonderful things through him.

聖靈一直在透過他做奇妙的事情。


Sick people were healed, blind eyes opened, food multiplied...!

病人痊癒了，瞎子睜開了眼睛，食物成倍增加......！


Stephen had watched in amazement

史蒂芬驚訝地看著


as the Holy Spirit had brought the Kingdom of God

因為聖靈帶來了神的國度


in a wonderful, miraculous way,

以一種奇妙、神奇的方式，


everywhere he went.

他走到哪裡。


When the Jews from Asia had accosted him in the marketplace,

當來自亞洲的猶太人在市集上與他爭論時，


the Holy Spirit had responded so eloquently! --

聖靈如此雄辯地回應了！--


Stephen could only marvel

史蒂芬只能驚歎


at the irrefutable words

在無可辯駁的話語中


coming out of

出來


his own mouth.

他自己的嘴。


💠



And now, this had happened.

現在，這已經發生了。


As the infuriated mob swept him roughly off his feet,

當憤怒的暴徒粗暴地把他從腳上掃下來時，


down the street

沿著街道


and into the Council Hall,

並進入理事會大廳，


Stephen could only trust

史蒂芬只能相信


the Holy Spirit

聖靈


was going before him.

在他前面走。


💠



Based on Acts 6:1-15; Matthew 10:17-20.

基於使徒行傳6:1-15；馬太福音10:17-20。

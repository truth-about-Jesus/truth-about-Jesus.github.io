48 - Epilogue

尾聲

After the day

之後


Stephen was stoned to death,

史蒂芬被石頭砸死了，


a terrible persecution broke out

一場可怕的迫害爆發了反對


against the believers in Jerusalem,

耶路撒冷的信徒，


spearheaded by the young fellow Stephen had noticed

這場迫害是由史蒂芬注意到的那個年輕人發起的


as his spirit had left his body:

當他的靈魂離開他的身體時：


a disciple of Gamaliel

迦瑪列的弟子


named Saul.

名叫掃羅。


💠



Saul was ferocious in his attempts to destroy

掃羅在試圖摧毀時很兇猛


the movement of the false Messiah.

假彌賽亞的運動。


With the Chief Priest's backing, he invaded gatherings and homes,

在大祭司的支援下，他入侵了集會和家庭，


dragging men and women off

拖著男人和女人


to prison and death,

進入監獄和死亡，


if, even under torture,

如果，即使在折磨下，


they refused to deny their Lord.

他們拒絕否認他們的主。


💠



As a result,

結果，


all of the believers in Jerusalem --

耶路撒冷的所有信徒——


except the Apostles --

除了使徒們——


were scattered throughout Judea and Samaria.

分散在猶太和撒瑪利亞。


But wherever they went,

但無論他們走到哪裡，


they proclaimed the Good News

他們宣佈了好訊息


of Jesus,

關於耶穌，


their risen Messiah,

他們復活的彌賽亞，


just as He'd predicted.

正如他預測的那樣。


💠



Titus 3:4-6 (NIV)

4 But when the kindness and love of God our Savior appeared,

5 he saved us, not because of righteous things we had done, but because of his mercy. He saved us through the washing of rebirth and renewal by the Holy Spirit,

6 whom he poured out on us generously through Jesus Christ our Savior.


Titus 3:4-6 (Chinese NET (T))

4 但到了 神我們救主的恩慈，和他向人所施的慈愛顯明的時候，

5 他便救了我們，並不是因我們自己作的義行，而是以他的憐憫為基礎，藉著新生的洗，和聖靈的更新。

6 聖靈就是 神藉著耶穌基督我們救主，厚厚澆灌在我們身上的。

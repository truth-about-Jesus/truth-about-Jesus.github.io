39 - Ananias

阿納尼亞斯

At last, it was his turn.

終於輪到他了。


Ananias stepped forward and laid the shekels --

阿納尼亞斯走上前去，放下了錢——


half of what he had received from selling a field to his foolish neighbour! --

那隻是他把田地賣給愚蠢鄰居所得金額的一半！--


at the Apostle Peter's feet.

在使徒彼得的腳下。


"We sold a field, and offer the full proceeds to support the needy among the Messiah's followers," he said,

他說，「我們賣掉了一片田地，現在，我們將全部收益捐給您，用於幫助彌賽亞信徒中的貧困者。」


as Joe Barnabas had done.

正如喬·巴納巴斯所做的那樣。


That ought to buy him a little favour and influence with the leaders of the Messiah's movement!

這應該讓他在彌賽亞運動的主管人中獲得一點恩惠和影響力！


💠



The Holy Spirit bristled, filling Peter with honest indignation,

聖靈怒髮衝冠，讓彼得充滿了真誠的憤慨，


then spoke gently through him.

然後透過他悄然說話。


"Ananias, how has Satan so filled your heart

「阿納尼亞斯，撒旦是如何填滿你的心的


to lie to the Holy Spirit,

對聖靈撒謊，


and conceal a portion

並隱藏一部分


of the proceeds of the sale?"

銷售收益？」


The amount was not the question,

金額不是問題，


as Peter well knew

正如彼得所熟知的那樣


from his Master's teaching on giving.

源自於他師父關於給予錢的教導。


But the lie

但是謊言


was a problem.

是個問題。


💠



"Wasn't the field yours

「田地不是你的嗎


before it was sold?

在它被賣掉之前？


And once sold, the money received was still yours -- to do with as you pleased.

一旦售出，收到的錢仍然是你的——隨心所欲。


How could you think to do such a thing?

你怎麼會想到做這種事？


You haven't lied to men,

你沒有對男人撒謊，


but to God!"

但卻對上帝說謊！」


As soon as the words were out of Peter's mouth,

彼得一說出這些話，


Ananias

阿納尼亞斯


collapsed.

摔倒了。


💠



Based on Acts 4:32-37; Acts 5:1-11; Mark 12:41-44.

基於使徒行傳4:32-37；使徒行傳5:1-11；馬可福音12:41-44。

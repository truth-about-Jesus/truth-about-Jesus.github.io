28 - Jailed again

再次入獄

"Open up in there!"

「打開這扇門！」


Someone was pounding at the door.

有人在猛烈地敲門。


John got up sleepily and, without thinking, opened the door

約翰昏昏欲睡地起床，不假思索地開啟了門


to a sudden, chaotic invasion of torches, leather, weapons --

火炬、皮革、武器的突然、混亂的入侵——


and rough treatment.

和動粗。


When the dust had settled, all of Jesus' chosen Apostles were in jail --

當塵埃落定時，耶穌選擇的所有使徒都在監獄裡——


with armed guards standing before doors

武裝警衛站在門前


firmly bolted from the outside.

從外面牢牢地栓住。


💠



Caiaphas took his time calling together the Ruling Council.

該亞法並沒有急於統召集治理事會議。


There was no rush, now that the main followers of the false Messiah were safely ensconced away from their adoring -- he grimaced -- public.

既然假彌賽亞的主要追隨者已被安全地監禁起來，遠離了崇拜他們的公眾——他做了個鬼臉——那就不用著急了。


Let them stew in jail!

讓他們待在監獄裡！


The Council would be convened when he was good and ready.

理事會將在他準備好並準備好時召開。


💠



John ate a little of the bread the two Marys had brought to the jail.

約翰吃了一點兩位馬利亞帶到監獄的麵包。


Although he could fast, he appreciated their loving gesture to support the Apostles during their imprisonment.

雖然他可以禁食，但他很欣賞他們在使徒被監禁期間支援他們的慈愛姿態。


He'd overheard the latest guards saying the Council was to be convened in the morning.

他無意中聽到新來的衛兵說，事會將於明天早上召開。


The circumstances of their imprisonment had not been pleasant,

他們被監禁的情況並不愉快，


but he trusted the Holy Spirit would give them yet another opportunity

但他相信聖靈會再給他們一次機會


to testify boldly to Jesus' resurrection.

大膽地見證耶穌的復活。


💠



The cell door creaked open.

牢房的門吱呀一聲開了。


John sat up drowsily.

約翰昏昏欲睡地坐了起來。


It was still dark... not time for their hearing yet...?

天還黑......現在就進行審判還為時過早......？


A bright light entered the cell, and the Apostles shielded sleep-infused eyes.

明亮的光線進入牢房，使徒們遮蔽了充滿睡眠的眼睛。


"Hurry, get up!" commanded the luminous being.

「快點，起來！」 指揮發光的存在。


Confused yet obedient, many imagining they were merely dreaming,

困惑但聽話，許多人想象他們只是在做夢，


they followed the angel out the door,

他們跟著天使出了門，


around the guards --

在警衛周圍——


who didn't seem to notice them! --

誰似乎沒有注意到他們！--


and outside into the street.

然後走到外面，到街上去。


"Go," said the angel to them all.

「去吧，」天使對他們所有人說。


"Stand in the Temple, and speak to the people

「站在聖殿裡，向人們講話


all these words of life."

所有這些生命之言。」

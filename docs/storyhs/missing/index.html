30 - Missing

失蹤

Full of curiosity, the Chief Priest frowned, then gestured to the guard, who came forward and saluted respectfully.

祭司長充滿了好奇心，皺起了眉頭，然後向警衛示意，警衛上前恭敬地敬禮。


"Please give your report to the Council."

「請向理事會說你的報告。」


The guard spoke nervously.

警衛緊張地說話。


"We went to the prison and found it carefully locked up, and the guards standing before bolted doors;

「我們去了監獄，發現它被小心翼翼地鎖上了，警衛們站在鎖著的門前；


but when we opened them --" he gulped,

但當我們打開門時——」他焦急地說，


"there was no one inside!"

「裡面沒有人！」


💠



"What?!"

「什麼？！」


Caiaphas gave the guard a hard stare.

該亞法狠狠地盯著警衛。


"No one there!?"

「那裡沒有人！？」


A brief pause... then, more menacingly:

短暫的停頓......然後，更具有威脅性：


"Are you certain?"

「你確定嗎？」


"Yes, Your Excellency."

「是的，閣下。」


The guard bowed his head, feeling somehow as though the bad news was his fault.

警衛低下了頭，總覺得壞訊息是他的錯。


Now the entire Council chamber fell silent, as all pondered in perplexity.

現在，整個理事會廳都沉默了，所有人都在困惑中沉思。


What could have happened

究竟遭遇了什麼


to the false Messiah's followers?

那些假彌賽亞的追隨者們？


💠



Just then, one of Caiaphas' household servants entered,

就在這時，該亞法的一個僕人進來了，


went straight to the Chief Priest,

直接去找祭司長，


and whispered in his ear.

在他耳邊低語。


Caiaphas threw up his hands, then gestured toward the Council.

該亞法舉起雙手，然後向理事會做了個手勢。


"Tell them," he said

「告訴他們，」他說


with resignation.

無奈地。
